INSTITUTES

The High Institute of Computer
and Information Systems

As one of the leading higher institutes in the Arab Republic of Egypt, Abu Qir Higher Institutes were established in 1998 in line with the Egyptian government's policy to encourage private education, as the Egyptian Supreme Council of Universities and Private Institutes issued their licenses, which are valid as of the academic year 2000/2001.


And it offers a set of distinguished educational programs carefully designed to keep pace with contemporary changes and challenges. These programs focus on the practical application during the educational process, which helps the student to acquire the necessary tools to develop his intellectual and cognitive skills and prepare him as a distinguished graduate who is able to keep pace with the requirements of the labor market


The High Institute for Tourism,
Hotels and Antiquities Restoration

It awards a bachelor's degree in majors: Bachelor of Tourism Studies Division (Tourism Business Administration - Aviation Partnerships Management - Navigation Company Management - Special Events Management - Sports Event Management) Bachelor of Hospitality Management Division (Hotel Management - Restaurant Management - Air and Marine Hospitality Services Management - Culinary Arts Management - Customer Service Management - Special Events Management) Bachelor's degree in Tourist Guidance



The institute awards a bachelor's degree in antiquities (restoration of antiquities) in the following specializations (restoration of inorganic buildings and artifacts - the restoration of organic artifacts - the restoration of paintings)
