ABOUT US

As one of the leading higher institutes in the Arab Republic of Egypt, Abu Qir Higher Institutes were established in 1998 in line with the Egyptian government's policy to encourage private education, as the Egyptian Supreme Council of Universities and Private Institutes issued their licenses, which are valid as of the academic year 2000/2001.


And it offers a set of distinguished educational programs carefully designed to keep pace with contemporary changes and challenges. These programs focus on the practical application during the educational process, which helps the student to acquire the necessary tools to develop his intellectual and cognitive skills and prepare him as a distinguished graduate who is able to keep pace with the requirements of the labor market


The institutes accept students who are candidates from the coordination of high school and Azharite and students of technical diplomas and those who have failed to coordinate, with a maximum of one year, and transfers from colleges and institutes, and are subject to the coordination conditions of the Ministry of Higher Education and by the decision of the equivalence. ‚Äè

The institutes are also distinguished by a group of distinguished material and human capabilities such as computer labs, language laboratories, school stands equipped with the latest educational facilities, educational kitchen, educational hotel, educational restaurant, educational tourism companies, educational museum, antiquities restoration laboratories, studio, football stadium , Basketball court, volleyball court, five-a-side football field, gymnasium, cafeterias, libraries, data show halls, electronics labs, hard-ware laboratories.
